TAXI


Taxi:
Delhi is the only city in India which has taxicabs running only on Compressed Natural Gas. To hail a taxicab, you normally just wait on the street or go to locations called taxi stands. Taxicabs are referred to as taxis in Delhi and the word cab is rarely used.

The fares charged by taxi operators in the national Capital Territory of Delhi are as under:
Taxis(Black & Yellow Top) Rs 25/- for first Kilometer (upon downing the meter) and thereafter Rs. 14.00/- per Kilometer for Non-AC taxis and Rs . 16.00/ per Kilometer for AC taxis for every additional Kilometer
Night Charges25% of the fare (11:00 PM to 5:00 AM)
Waiting ChargesRs 30/- per hour or part thereof (Subject to a minimum of 15 minutes stay)
LuggageRs. 10/- shall be charged as extra luggage charges whereas the driver/ operator shall not charge and money for a shopping bag or a small suitcase

Call 91-11-42400400 or SMS at 56767 to register a complaint regarding misbehavior by TSR/Taxi drivers
